Games

Dec 9, 2022

Ndemic created 2 games, Plague Inc. And Rebel Inc.
Plague Inc.:
Plague Inc. is a game about creating a plague that will make humans extinct. You start with a simple bacteria and work your way up to other diseases, or you can just buy the diseases for some dollars. You can recreate real-world scenarios and test it.
Rebel Inc.:
You create revolutions and rebel Cure Inc.:
Cure Inc. is a game inside Plague Inc. where instead of killing everyone, you be the hero and save everyone, or be the last survivors and let anyone infected die
